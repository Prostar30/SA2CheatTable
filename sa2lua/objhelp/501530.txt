This is a GUN Beetle (floating) robot.

Z rotation = frequency of "hovering"

X scale = type of robot

0 - Basic
1 - Basic (moves slowly)
2 - Electric (Y scale controls shield drop time)
3 - Electric (moves slowly when shield is down)
4 - Gun
5 - Cloaked (appears when you get close)
6 - Spring
7 - Bomb (Y scale controls delay between bombs)
